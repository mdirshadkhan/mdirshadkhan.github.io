السَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ


(As-Salam Alaykoum - Peace Be Upon You)

Who are Mahdavis?   Mahdavis are sunni muslims who believe that prophet Syed Muhammad ( pbuh ) is the last prophet of Islam Are followers of Syed Muhammad Jaunpuri Mahdi al Ma’ ood who is from the progeny of prophet Muhammad( pbuh), as the promised Mahdi as mandated by Allah SWT in holy Quran and fulfilling the prophecy of prophet Muhammad (s. a. s) about the Advent of Mahdi Alaihis Salam, Who also believe in the second coming of Esa Alaihis Salam (Jesus) before the Day of Judgement. Mahdavis predominantly reside in India, Pakistan, Afghanistan etc. and some of them have migrated to different countries like USA, UK, CANADA, AUSTRALIA etc in search of livelihood and better avenues for themselves and for their families thereby also fulfilling the obligation of HIJRAT for the sake of spread of ISLAM. Our Aim: We , here in Canada are spread over the provinces of Ontario, BC, Alberta etc. This website is one of our endeavours to bring the Canadian Mahdavis under one platform and keep all of us updated about the religious and related affairs of our community so that we can lead our lives with same religious fervour and obligation as back home.
